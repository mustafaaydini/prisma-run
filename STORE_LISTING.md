## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Run, dodge, and match your way across an ever-expanding prism universe!

PRISMA RUN is a fast endless runner with a twist: you're a living prism who
must shift color to match glowing gates as you race down an infinite,
winding road. Chain the same color for combo multipliers, dodge crystal
obstacles, and grab power-ups to survive as long as you can.

FEATURES
• 5 hand-crafted worlds that loop and get harder: Dusk Valley, Crystal
  Caverns, Neon City, Cosmic Void, Solar Dawn
• Simple one-thumb controls: swipe to change lanes, tap to shift color
• Combo system rewards matching streaks with growing score multipliers
• Power-ups: Shield, Magnet, and Star Frenzy
• Collect shards to unlock skins (Comet Orb, Stardart, Nova Star, Crown
  Jewel) and permanent upgrades in the shop
• Daily rewards and rotating missions
• Fully offline — play anywhere, no internet required after install
