Prisma Run — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Prisma Run does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server.

What the game stores

Your best score, shards, unlocked skins, upgrades, and mission progress are saved only on your own device, using the browser's local storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Ads

If a rewarded-ad network (e.g. CrazyGames, Poki, or AdMob) is integrated in the version you are using, that network may collect data per its own privacy policy in order to serve ads. Check the store listing of the specific build you installed for which ad network, if any, is active.

Permissions

The game may request device vibration for haptic feedback. No camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage permissions are used.
